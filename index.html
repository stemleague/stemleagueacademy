Frequently Asked Questions

What is STEM League Academy?

STEM League Academy (previously Developer and Explorer Program) is a virtual learning community that offers year-round coding classes and mentorship for beginner and intermediate middle and high school students.

STEM League was founded during Summer 2020 in Bellevue, Washington to help students learn to code during the pandemic. Since 2020, STEM League has taught and mentored over 600+ students and received support and recognition from UW Seattle, Microsoft, and T-Mobile. STEM League's Founders Elizabeth Lin, Christin Lin, and Sophia Lin and their growing mentor team are motivated to continue spreading STEM League's mission to "Educate and Inspire student to Pursue STEM."

How much does it cost to attend STEM League Academy?

Our program costs vary depending on the learning track and duration/type of the program. Please see our program application for the cost of each program.

Can students outside of US or Canada apply?

No, we only accept students who are located in US and Canada time zones.

Is this a virtual program?

Yes, STEM League Academy Programs are held virtually.

What is the age requirement to apply?

Students must be in grades 6-12th grade to apply (rising 6th graders are eligible).

What will I get out of this program? Why should I learn to code?

This program is your opportunity to learn coding skills to build your own personal projects and gain valuable mentorship from Mentors and Instructors. Our mission is to "Educate and Inspire students to Pursue STEM", and we aim to equip you with technical and interpersonal skills for you to build your STEM journey.
