Tech World & Beyond • Panel

This week, we will be hearing from a Software Development Engineer from Expedia Group speak about her experiences in her job and being a Mentor in the Girls Who Code Program. She will also share about her insights of seeing tech evolve in the past decade and some tips on for you to keep refining your coding skills and pursuing your tech interests. Additionally, our panel moderator will also be talking about some potential questions you can ask your mentor to keep a lasting authentic mentor-mentee relationship even after this program ends!
