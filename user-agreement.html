STEM League+ User Agreement


We expect all users of STEM League+ to follow to following guidelines when interacting with our materials.

STEM League+ is a platform that provides students access to text and code developed by STEM League Instructors and Staff. This includes lessons, readings, slides, and workshops materials. These materials (media, text, code) are property of STEM League. Students or volunteers may not copy and/or use STEM League property for their own personal profit. Any unauthorized copy of STEM League+ Curriculum Material or unauthorized reuse of STEM League+ Curriculum Material will not be tolerated.

Students who are learning with STEM League+ and Developers enrolled in Developer Program may use our program materials to build their own portfolio (crediting STEM League) for their career exploration including but not limited to applying to internships, camps, and other programs. Our goal is that students use STEM League+ as a learning platform to leverage their own STEM career and future opportunities.
