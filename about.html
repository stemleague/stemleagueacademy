About Us


STEM League+ is a learning service offered by STEM League. Launched in 2021 after the success of the Developer Program, it provides an interactive learning platform for young students to learn coding and STEM-related topics. We are currently releasing new content each month.

We require STEM League+ users to be subscribed or be enrolled as a student in the Explorer or Developer Program in order to gain access to our full curriculum. We encourage support from our community in order to continue providing accessible STEM Education for students of all backgrounds.

Elizabeth Lin, the Co-Founder of STEM League, developed STEM League+ and manages all content updates to the platform. If you have any questions or are interested in a partnership with STEM League, please reach out to us over email at stemleague18@gmail.com.
