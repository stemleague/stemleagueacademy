Inspired by "Brown Bag Lunches," this series is developed to bring students into a conversation-style session where speakers share various topics in computing applications and interact with students through Q & A.

Episodes

    

Details

  • 1. Day in the Life of a Software Engineer (44m)
    Hear from Software Engineers at Microsoft and Lyft about steps you can take to pursue a career in software engineering.
  • 2. Imagineering, a Fireside Chat (59m)
    A current intern at Disney will share her experience in STEM through a fun conversation with Developers.
  • 3. Computing and Accessibility Talk by UW CSE (62m)
    Renowned UW Computer Science Professor, Richard Ladner share his research in assistive technology.
  • 4. DNA Data Storage by UW CSE
    Learn about Computer Science Research from Microsoft Senior Researcher working on DNA Data Storage applications at UW CSE Department.
  • 5. Fireside Chat with Product Manager from Roblox
    Jump into converation with Isabel, a Product Manager at Roblox and a 2021 STEM League Mentor.
