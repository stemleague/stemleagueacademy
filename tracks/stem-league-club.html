How to Start a STEM League Club

All Developer alumni are eligible to start a STEM League Club Chapter in their local community/school, where they utilize the resources from STEM League+ (curriculum, videos, etc.) to start their own club and teach other students to code. Our goal is for students to continue spreading STEM Education while gaining leadership experience that will boost them for future college applications and job opportunities. This series will include monthly episodes providing guidance on how to lead STEM League Clubs and leadership advice.
