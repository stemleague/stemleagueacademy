We will guide you through how to prepare for your future career in STEM,
which includes professional development workshops and tech talks from
industry folks to build your techincal and interpersonal skills.

Episodes

    

Details

  • 1. Make Your Own Portfolio (19m)
    Wanna share your projects (music, pictures, gamer achievements) on an app and website interface? Join this workshop to learn how to showcase your work!
  • 2. Intern Panel (20m)
    Two awesome interns from Microsoft are sharing their advice on how to navigate the STEM field & tips to become a great Developer! :)
  • 3. T-Mobile Magenta Career Talk
    Learn how to get internships and STEM opportunities at T-Mobile and dive into the amazing culture that surrounds an expansive 5G technology empire.
  • 4. Hosting Projects on Github (13m)
    Learn how to use Git and Command Line to push your own projects (coding sessions, workshops, personal hackathon project) to your own GitHub!
  • 5. Web APIs
    Dive into using Web APIs for your applications, how computers and machines connect with each other to transfer data across the web.
  • 6. Career Ready (25m)
    It is time to get your game on & prep yourself for a career in tech! We will be sharing with you our insights on personal branding, resume building, and finding STEM opportunities!

Career Development

Launching a career whether that in high school or college focusing in STEM can be challenging. We provide students insights on how to excel in their technical skills through mentorship, coding skills, and leadership in the STEM World.
